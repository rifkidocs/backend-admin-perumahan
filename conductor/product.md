# Initial Concept
The user wants to build/manage a backend system for a housing project (Perumahan).

# Product Guide - Housing Project Management System

## Vision
To provide a comprehensive backend management system for housing development projects, centralizing operational, financial, and administrative processes to improve efficiency and project transparency.

## Target Audience
- **Administrators and Management:** Project Owners and Managers who need high-level oversight and reporting.
- **Operational Staff:** Teams across Sales, Marketing, Human Resources, and Finance who manage day-to-day business activities.

## Core Business Goals
- **Streamline Housing Project Management:** Modernize how housing projects are planned, tracked, and delivered by providing real-time visibility into progress and timelines.
- **Operational Excellence:** Centralize fragmented processes across different departments into a single, cohesive Strapi-powered backend.

## Key Feature Areas

### 1. Housing Project & Inventory Management
- **Units and Inventory:** Comprehensive tracking of housing units, including availability, specifications, and status updates.
- **Timeline & Progress:** Monitoring of daily and weekly construction progress across various project phases.
- **Document Control:** Centralized management for technical documents, permits (IMB/PBG), licenses, and site plans.

### 2. Sales & Marketing Operations
- **CRM & Leads:** Management of marketing leads, customer communication logs, and follow-up schedules.
- **Booking & Finance:** Streamlining the reservation process, payment invoicing, and tracking consumer accounts receivable (Piutang).
- **Promotional Assets:** Managing marketing collateral such as videos, brochures, and active promotional campaigns.

### 3. HR & Operational Management
- **Workforce Management:** Tracking employee attendance, managing shifts, and handling leave requests.
- **Security Operations:** Specialized support for security guard attendance including 12-hour/overnight shifts and flexible patrol reporting with multi-entry logs (photos and location verification).
- **Performance Evaluation:** Systematized KPI tracking, performance reviews, and employee ratings.
- **Supply Chain & Logistics:** Managing warehouse inventory, material requests, supplier evaluations, and procurement workflows.
